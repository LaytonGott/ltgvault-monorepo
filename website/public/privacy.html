Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Overview

LTG Vault ("we", "our", or "us") operates the ltgvault.com website and the ChapterGen Chrome Extension. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

ChapterGen Chrome Extension

The ChapterGen Chrome Extension is designed to help you grab transcripts from YouTube videos. Here's what you need to know:

Data Collection:

  • The extension does NOT collect any personal information
  • The extension does NOT track your browsing history
  • The extension does NOT store any data on external servers

How It Works:

  • When you click "Grab Transcript", the extension reads the transcript text from the current YouTube page
  • The transcript is temporarily stored in your browser's local storage
  • When you click "Send to ChapterGen", the transcript is sent to ltgvault.com for processing
  • The transcript data is used solely to generate chapters and is not stored permanently

Permissions:

  • activeTab: Required to read transcript content from the current YouTube tab
  • scripting: Required to extract transcript text from YouTube's interface
  • Host permissions (youtube.com, ltgvault.com): Required to function on YouTube pages and send data to ChapterGen
